Getting Started

  1. Go to environment where you can focus
  2. Get a white board, this could just be a pen and paper
  3. Click on the Questions Tab
  4. Refine questions you want to test yourself with.
  5. Click on a question
  6. White board the question given to you.
  7. Look at Solution to compare results
  8. Watch Video if necessary to get further exclamation
  9. Repeat this process to further improve your white boarding skills
